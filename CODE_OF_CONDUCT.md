# Code of conduct

## The short version

Be decent to people. This is a small project about a microphone; nothing here is worth being cruel over.

## What's expected

- Assume good faith. Most disagreements are misunderstandings.
- Critique code, not people. "This allocates on the audio thread" is useful; "this is garbage" is not.
- Accept that maintainers may say no. Scope is a feature.
- Respect that contributors are volunteers with jobs, families, and finite patience.

## What's not acceptable

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, sexualized content, doxxing, or sustained
disruption of discussions. This applies to issues, pull requests, commit messages, and any other
project space.

## Enforcement

Report problems to the maintainers via a private message or by opening a confidential security advisory
if the content is sensitive. Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete contributions, and may block accounts
that don't respect this document. We'll aim to be proportionate and to explain ourselves.
